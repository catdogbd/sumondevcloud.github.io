Brand Identity
Brand identity is a term that describes how the various brands we all know and love operate, in our everyday lives. It refers to who owns what with whom because it isn't about "you" or even corporate ownership; you own something if you're part of the company itself. A corporate identity or corporate image is the manner in which a corporation, firm or business enterprise presents itself to the public. The corporate identity is typically visualized by branding and with the use of trademarks, but it can also include things like product design, advertising, public relations etc
